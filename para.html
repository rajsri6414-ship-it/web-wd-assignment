Welcomes to ABES Engineering College

ABES Engineering College, located in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, is a premier private institution known for its commitment to academic excellence and strong placement records Established in 2000 and affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), the college is accredited by NAAC with an 'A' grade and the NBA for various programs, reflecting its high educational standards.ABES Engineering College boasts a sprawling campus with modern infrastructure designed to foster a conducive learning environment.The 17-acre campus provides a modern learning environment with state-of-the-art facilities, including air-conditioned classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, an extensive library, and comprehensive sports amenities like a floodlit cricket stadium and a swimming pool.
