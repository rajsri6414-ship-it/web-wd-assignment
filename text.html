ABES Engineering College, situated in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh is a distinguished private institution recognized for its dedication to academic excellence and impressive placement records. Established in 2000 and affiliated withDr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), the college holds accreditation from NAAC with an 'A' grade and the NBA for various programs, underscoring its commitment to high educational standards. The college features a sprawling 17-acre campus equipped with modern infrastructure designed to create a conducive learning environment. Facilities include air-conditioned classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, an extensive library, and comprehensive sports amenities such as a floodlit cricket stadium and a swimming pool.
ABES offers a wide array of undergraduate and postgraduate courses, with B.Tech programs in fields like Computer Science, AI & Machine Learning, and Data Science being particularly sought after. The institution emphasizes holistic development through a vibrant club culture and a dedicated placement cell (Center for Career Planning and Development), which attracts leading recruiters such as Amazon, Microsoft, TCS, and Infosys. Notably, the highest package in 2024 reached INR 59.91 LPA. Overall, ABES is considered an excellent choice for students seeking a disciplined academic environment coupled with promising career opportunities in the Delhi-NCR region.
